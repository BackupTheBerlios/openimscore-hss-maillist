So, as others have responded, regular expressions can not be used in the 
Request-URI SPT.

Another alternative that was not mentioned is that you can define a 
wildcarded PSI that would match ws*@open-ims.test. But this will also 
match for ws1 and ws2, so careful. Better use then ws??????@open-ims.test
