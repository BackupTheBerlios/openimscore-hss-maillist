Hi,

of course this can be done with any language, where you can speak with
a MySQL-Server.

Please bear in mind: The Fraunhofer HSS / FHoSS is for research and
development and not for real-life deployment. Thus it scales not very
well (it doesn't have to) and the Webfrontend is the only way to add
users    "normally". You should not use it for real-life
deployments...
Other HSS's will have a proper interface for mass deployments.

Carsten
