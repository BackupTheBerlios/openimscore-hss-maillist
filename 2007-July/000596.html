Hello Beatriz,

The other two should be filled when the request is sent out with the
values of the Diameter filled End-to-End and Hop-by-Hop IDs. These ids
are supposed to be unique, so that when a answer (PNA) comes back it
could be identified and the row removed. Of course, the transaction
state could also be kept in memory by the Diameter stack, but with this
field the communication is theoretically safer in case of restarts...

-Dragos
