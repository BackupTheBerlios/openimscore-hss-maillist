Hi Asma,

It seems that you have a different application assigned for your
specified IP and port and these cannot be used anymore for FHoSS. As you
can see yourself in the logs your error is:
"
 java.net.BindException: Cannot assign requested address:8080
"
Please change the combination of IP & port value with a free one, and
try again.

Adrian
