Hi fancy_xiao,

I observed that you are not providing an SIP-Number-Auth-Items, nor a
SIP-Auth-Data-Item. I think this is your problem; although these are
optional in the specs, at the time of implementation these were
considered mandatory in FHoSS.
Are you testing the HSS with other Diameter client that the
openimscore's CSCFs? If yes, you have two options: include that headers
in the request or make a patch for hss and remove that fields from
mandatory to optional.

- -Adrian
