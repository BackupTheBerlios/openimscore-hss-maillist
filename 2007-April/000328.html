Hi jp_liu,

In my opinion the UE should not know the mechanism that is used in HSS
related to generation of the new SQN values. The client holds an array
with previously accepted SQNs and use the conditions defined in C.2 (of
the same 33.102) to figure out if the SQN can be accepted or not. As you
can see, for all the situations (time base, partialy time base or not
time based) the conditions for the client are the same. Only on the HSS
side things are different.

- -Adrian
