Hi Arief,

what you can do for recording session details (as duration of a multimedia session or for events like messaging) is that you equip your application server with the appropriate (IMS) charging interfaces. For the used bytes, you are stuck without a PCC. As Franz said: the Open IMS Core does not offer charging support as of now.
