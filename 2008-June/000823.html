Hi Raphael,

> The examples of Registrar that I've found for Mobicents are based on the
> Ip adress of clients. 

But there is another fact: You cannot send an initial request into IMS with
an IP-address instead of a domain-name. The P-CSCF and S-CSCF will not
handle such a request.

> Of course I can adapt the Registrar to force it to
> use URI instead of IP. 

This would be necessary.

> But I would like also to be able to exclude IMS
> from a dialog between 2 clients managed by Mobicents. The client asks to
> Ims to engage a communication, which it transfers to Mobicents which will
> manage the communication without IMS thanks to its knowledge of the IP
> adress of clients. But here is my problem.

So you are building a separate architecture besides IMS and you only need
IMS to get the IP-address of the clients. Be aware that you usually cannot
reach the client registered in IMS by bypassing the P-CSCF.
