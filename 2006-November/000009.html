Hi,

there is no plugin needed, just make sure that you have the proper 3GPP
files referenced in the /usr/share/wireshark/wireshark/diameter/ folder
(imscxdx.xml and TGPPSh.xml) and the needed libxml2-dev package (that's
at least for Debian) installed.
