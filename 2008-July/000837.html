Hi Eivar,

Yes, it is implementation specific. The reasons go from lack of 
resources in building complex push-models in the HSS to avoiding storms 
of messages.

Anyway, check the hss.properties and change the SH_NOTIF_CHECK_INTERVAL 
to a lower value if you wish... but be aware that this is the interval 
at which a table will be polled for changes... so don't do it too often 
or you'll loose performance.

Cheers,
-Dragos
