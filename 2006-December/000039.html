Hi Jamal,

Please try to run all the steps, once at a time and give me the errors
and the step in which these occurred.

1. Make an "ant cleanall" for cleaning all the binaries and the src-gen

2. ant gen - after this step take a look in the src-gen and look for
folders: de/fhg/fokus/sh, de/fhg/fokus/cx and de/fhg/fokus/zh. If any of
these is not there, here is the problem...but you should receive some
error before.

3. ant compile

4. ant deploy

If you encounter problems during a step, please send to me that error
and specify also the step. I will have a look on it. I guess that you
have a problem somewhere in step 2, or maybe 3.

Bye,
Adrian
