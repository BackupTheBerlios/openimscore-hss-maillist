Hello Gaurav,

I am thinking that you didn't compile the sources with java 1.5. Be
aware, that even you have java 1.5 installed on your machine, your
compiler settings can be changed, to compile your sources also as java
1.4 or as 1.3. So, verify again your compiler settings. To force your
compilation to 1.5 you can specify this in build.xml, at javac section.
Add compiler="javac1.5" attribute to javac.

Also, another possibility is that you have more versions of java
installed on your machine, and "ant" see your other version, not the 1.5
version. If you use linux, look on JAVA_HOME variable that is used by
ant. (e.g. echo $JAVA_HOME) If is not pointing to your java1.5 change it
with export.

I am almost sure, that one of these is the cause for your problem.

Adrian
