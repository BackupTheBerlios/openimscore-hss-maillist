Franz, Peter,

To me this looks more like a Hibernate issue than the HSS logic itself.

A bit of Googling for "org.hibernate.SessionException: Session is closed" leads to a wealth of information.

http://www.google.com/search?q=org.hibernate.SessionException%3A+Session+is+closed&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8&rls=org.mozilla:en-US:official&client=firefox-a

A couple of links suggest that this could be due to mismatched jar files for hibernate-annotations.jar.

Franz - Do you have the hibernate framework installed on your notebook due to previous experiments? This could be a possible avenue of investigation.
