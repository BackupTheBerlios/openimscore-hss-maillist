Hi George,

It looks like you are trying to connect to 127.0.1.1:3870 but there is 
nothing running there. If you still want your /etc/hosts configured on 
127.0.1.1, please update the <Accept> tag in the DiameterPeerHSS.xml, 
icscf.xml and scscf.xml accordingly (the diameter nodes are listening on 
127.0.0.1:3868,3869,3870 by default).

-Dragos
