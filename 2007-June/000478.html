Hi,

> What is the usual method for telecoms to provision users on their own
> network? Is there a standard interface to HSS that telecoms can use?

There are a lot more issues in managing telecom networks than provisioning
users at HSS, because there are a many more components in a real network.
The TeleManagement-Forum (http://www.tmforum.org/) is an organisation to
address all these issues. There is the concept of eTOM (Enhanced Telecom
Operations Map) to go into more details.

In principle it is important to have a "northbound" interface to connect
network elements (element managers) to an overall management centre. SOAP is
actually a well accepted interface for that task.
