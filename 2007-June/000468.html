The FHoSS can be connected with more then 4 DiameterPeers without any problems. 
There are Diameter configure files both at FHoSS and at DiameterPeers connected to the FHoSS. Have you modified them correctly?

e.g. at FHoSS:

 <Peer FQDN="icscf.open-ims.test" Realm="open-ims.test" port="3869">
 <Peer FQDN="scscf.open-ims.test" Realm="open-ims.test" port="3870">
 <Peer FQDN="scscf1.open-ims.test" Realm="open-ims.test" port="3871">
 <Peer FQDN="scscf2.open-ims.test" Realm="open-ims.test" port="3872">


at S-CSCF1: 
 <DiameterPeer
  FQDN="scscf1.open-ims.test"
  ...
 >

 <Acceptor port="3871" bind="127.0.0.1">

at S-CSCF2:
 <DiameterPeer
  FQDN="scscf2.open-ims.test"
  ...
 >

 <Acceptor port="3872" bind="127.0.0.1">
