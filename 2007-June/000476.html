though I am not an expert on telecom OSS systems, I can say that the
FHoSS for now does not support any standardized means of user
provisioning. There is a script though that does the job quite well. The
web interface will mainly help to quickly build up service profiles and
to manage application server settings but of course lets you also
administrate users.
