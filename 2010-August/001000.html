> I have installed IMS Communicator(192.168.0.20) in windows and I have the
> Open IMS Core installed in UBUNTU(192.168.0.45).
> 
> When I tried sending register I get 403 Unknown User.

You have to use domain names and not numeric IP addresses.
IMS is based on domains.

A REGISTER-request 
	REGISTER sip:192.168.0.45 SIP/2.0
cannot be handled by IMS.
