Yes.
The most easiest way to repair the misconfiguration (out of my experience)
is to delete the database tables 
    	  drop database icscf;

    	  drop database hss_db;
and restart your installation at Step 4: Configure the Environment.
Log what you have done.

If the problem then still exists your log should be analyzed.
