Hi Pankaj,

you would either have to configure the P-CSCF to insert the domain
"open-ims.test" in your  P-Visited-Network-ID (currently it seems to be
set to "Visited network number 1" ) or you configure a new domain in the
HSS and add that to the roaming networks of the IMPI bob at open-ims.test 

I overlooked that in the first mail ... sorry.
