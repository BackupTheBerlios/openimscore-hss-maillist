Hi all,

 

We are using the openimscore ims network to do some interoperability test.
We are using the P-CSCF, the I-CSCF, the S-CSCF and the Sip2Ims network
elements.

As we are developing our own HSS, we have replaced the FoHSS by our HSS.

We use X-Lite as the UA to generate the Sip register message.

 

Every thing goes well until we get to the authentication step.

We tried to exchange, SIP, UAR,UAA, SIP-Unathorized (401 UE challenge), SIP
(challenge), UAR-UAA, MAR-MAA message.

The first blocking point is in the SIP2IMS element which is not able to
authorize the User-Agent upon the SIP-Register message coming from the UA
after the challenge.

After having a look on the mysql sip2ims database, I saw that there is a
table "credentials" which contains ha1 and hab1 row. I assume that these
fields are use in the first authentication step made by the SIP2IMS. 

The thing we modified is that we are replaced the realm "open-ims.test" by
"127.0.0.1'". And I assume that "open-ims.test" has been used to generate
ha1 and hab1 key. 

Is it possible that the issue of that is to regenerate correct key of ha1
and hab1 with our realm (127.0.0.1) ?

If yes, could you please give us the way of generating theses key ?

 

But after having solve this problem, I think that the next problem will be
at the S-CSCF level when it will compare the SIP-Auth vector generated by
our HSS the the one expected from the UA. To generate the vector, we use a
private key which is provisioned in our HSS database. 

Could you please explains us the way of configured our HSS to make it
generate the SIP-Auth vectors expected by the S-CSCF ?

 

 

Thnaks you in advance.

Jean-Christophe.
