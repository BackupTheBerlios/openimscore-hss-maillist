Hi Zerin,


I use sailfin with OpenIMSCore and I use also Sh interface to store some
service information in the HSS. 

Sailfin doen't support Diameter conection but you can add
JavaDiameterPeer project to Sailfin. I did so and it works. You can use
JAvaDiameterPeer to create a Diameter command, send it to HSS and also
recieve another one and take the AVP you need and the information
inside.


Good luck!
