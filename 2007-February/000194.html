Hello Katty,

> It seems to me line 9 in ZhDataType.xsd, xs:import
> namespace=..., causes some confusion. After I comment
> out this line, FHoSS can be compiled successfully.

Probably the path specified in "schemaLocation" isn't the real one for
your installation; for me it works fine and were people on the mailing
list that used that FAQ and they solved their problem. As you said that
after commenting the import tag everything was ok, this imply that the
schemaLocation is not good, or your xml.xsd has some problems.
"xml.xsd" should not be modified at all. However, very strange that you
had to comment some lines in it. You are sure that you take it from this
link: http://www.w3.org/2001/xml.xsd ? It is very sure that is a valid
xml document, so no syntax errors should occur.

> After I comment
> out this line, FHoSS can be compiled successfully. Do
> you think if this will cause some side effects during
> test run?

No, it will not cause side effects. That schema is used by castor when
is generating the additional classes for Zh data types, but I think that
the xml.xsd schema is used only to make some validation stuff.

Bye,
Adrian
