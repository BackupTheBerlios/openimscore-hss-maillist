 Hello Katty,

 Are you sure that you didn't modified by mistake "xml.xsd"? Your error is saying that the "xs" prefix from your xml is not recognized and that has not a namespace associated to it. I suppose that you modified xml.xsd by mistake... if you do not have an internet connection, when you make "ant gen", you should modify the other xml file: "xsd/ZhDataType.xsd" as described here: http://www.openimscore.org/docs/faq.html#HSS
Tell me if this was your problem.
