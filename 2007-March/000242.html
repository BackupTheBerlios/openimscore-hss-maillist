Hi SRINI VASAN,

You have almost answered to your question :) If you cannot ping the
scscf.open-ims.test, icscf.open-ims.test or hss.open-ims.test is clear
that your core will not work.
You have still a dns configuration error somewhere. Please take a look
on the dns howto available here:
http://www.openimscore.org/docs/install.html#annexa
You probably forgot to add the sub-domains(scscf, icscf, hss) to the
dnszone file.

Cheers,
Adrian
