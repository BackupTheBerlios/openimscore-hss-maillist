Hi,

we just discussed about the SIP methods offered on HSS Web-interface for
iFC. According to 3GPP TS 23.218 only initial requests are required to be
used as service trigger points. And these are defined as:

Initial Request: a SIP request that either initiates the creation of a new
dialog or is part of a standalone transaction.

Therefore ACK and BYE should not be offered to our understanding.
