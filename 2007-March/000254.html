Hi Luis Silva,

The Origin-Host, Identity field should indicate the name of the host,
not the IP address. This name, then is resolved into the IP address. In
this way, the hss will know with which AS is speaking and what rights it
has regarding the Sh interface. I think this is the normal way.

Example:
AS1
sip:as1.open-ims.test:5070

AS2
sip:as2.open-ims.test:5080

The Identity field of the Origin-host will be as1.open-ims.test,
as2.open-ims.test respectively.
The IP address for the two names can be of course, the same.

Cheers,
Adrian
