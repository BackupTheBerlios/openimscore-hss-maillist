Hi i2cat,

I found the thread in the mailing lists in which Dragos talks about
triggering on the Request-URI.

Dragos says:

"Unfortunately, the 3GPP specs do not indicate the possibility of using 

regular expressions in the Request-URI. I find this strange, but it is 
implemented like this right now.

I would propose extensions, as this is often quite bothering. Now we 
triggered instead on the To header, as for headers you can use regular 
expression, but this is not so good."


And this is the complete thread:

http://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openimscore-users/2008-May/005498.html



Take a look, I think it will be useful for you.

Regards, 

Enrique
